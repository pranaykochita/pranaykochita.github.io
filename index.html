About

Industrial Capital & Strategic Advisory

CA Pranay Kochita is an Investment Banker and Transaction Advisor specializing in raising growth capital for high-growth Indian enterprises across manufacturing, technology, consumer, and clean-tech sectors. With a track record of over 150 forensic business evaluations and ₹100Cr+ in capital raised, Pranay de-risks capital entry by identifying scalability moats, structuring optimal funding architectures, and connecting ambitious founders with the right institutional partners.

His work spans the full transaction lifecycle — from financial model and investment memo construction, to investor outreach, due diligence facilitation, and deal closure — ensuring that companies are not merely funded, but structurally positioned for sustained growth.

Investment Thesis

ECI Systems is a high-margin equity story built on two decades of industrial automation excellence. Transitioning from a service provider to a multi-unit manufacturer with UL 508A and ISO 9001:2015 certifications, the firm has a unique export moat — shipping 70 tonnes of material monthly to the USA. Tier-1 System House relationships with Siemens, ABB, and Schneider position it as a primary beneficiary of Industry 4.0 and the global China Plus One strategy.

Market Opportunity

Capitalizing on India's projected $1.6T manufacturing GVA by 2034, ECI is capturing share in Semiconductor and Solar EPC sectors. UL/IEC compliance unlocks high-margin Western markets — specifically the USA's Oil & Gas and Semiconductor value chains.

Growth Capital Ask

Strategic capital to operationalize a 65,000 sq.ft. facility, scaling Semiconductor revenue from ₹20Cr (FY26) to ₹100Cr+ via alliances with YES Technologies and MindOx, while fulfilling the ₹26Cr Solar EPC order book.

Strategic Mandate

The Network Deal is a capital advisory mandate spanning a media enterprise with both listed and unlisted news network entities. The engagement covers structuring growth capital, identifying strategic investors, and facilitating transactions to scale the network's reach and monetization across digital and broadcast media.

Value Creation Thesis

Media networks with credible editorial presence and a dual listed/unlisted structure offer significant strategic value — both as acquisition targets for larger media conglomerates and as standalone platforms for institutional capital seeking media sector exposure. The mandate is designed to unlock this value through disciplined capital structuring and investor positioning.
