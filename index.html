Investment Thesis

ECI Systems represents a high-margin equity story built on two decades of industrial automation excellence. Tier-1 partnerships with Siemens, ABB, and Schneider position it as a primary beneficiary of Industry 4.0 and the global "China Plus One" strategy.


The firm's UL 508A and ISO 9001:2015 certifications enable access to high-margin Western markets, specifically within the USA's Oil & Gas and Semiconductor value chains.


Growth Verticals

Semiconductors: Strategic alliances with YES Technologies and MindOx Techno targeting ₹100Cr+ in long-term revenue.

Solar EPC: ₹26Cr order book for smart control and VCB panels, trajectory to exceed ₹50Cr in FY next.
