About

Industrial Capital and Strategic Advisory

CA Pranay Kochita is an Investment Banker and Transaction Advisor specialising in raising growth capital for high-growth Indian enterprises across manufacturing, technology, consumer, and clean-tech sectors. He bridges the analytical rigour of a Chartered Accountant with the deal-making perspective of a seasoned financial advisor.

With a track record of over 150 forensic business evaluations and Rs.100Cr+ in capital raised, Pranay de-risks capital entry by identifying scalability moats, structuring optimal funding architectures, and connecting ambitious founders with the right institutional partners.

His work spans the full transaction lifecycle — from financial model and investment memo construction, to investor outreach, due diligence facilitation, and deal closure — ensuring that companies are not merely funded, but structurally positioned for sustained growth.
